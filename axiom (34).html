About Axiom

Axiom is a minimal, voice-first assistant. It runs entirely in this browser tab: your API keys, chat, and settings are stored only in this browser's local storage.

Tap the orb to talk, or use the text fallback if speech input isn't available. Ask AXIOM to take a note or write something down, and it will save it to your Notes page — say "start a new note" or "add to that note" to control whether it starts fresh or continues. Ask it to schedule, cancel, or move something on a date, and it will update your Calendar page and jump you straight to it. Configure your AI provider and voice engine from Settings in the menu.

Use Brain to give AXIOM standing instructions you want it to always remember — like "keep replies short" or "always suggest a follow-up question." Add, edit, or remove them any time and they'll apply to every conversation from then on.
